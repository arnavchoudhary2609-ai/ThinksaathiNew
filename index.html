Thought Clarity Tool

Guided cognitive reframing to untangle spirals and regain calm perspective.

1
2
3
4

Step 1: Write Down the Worry

Venting your raw, unedited thoughts on paper gets the spiral out of your head. What's the looping loop that won't stop playing?

Step 2: Separate Facts from Assumptions

Our overthinking mind loves to treat scary assumptions as solid facts. Let's separate them. What is the actual, unarguable truth here? What is just a scary 'what-if' guess?

Step 3: Reframe Your Perspective

If your best friend came to you with this exact same worry, what warm, supportive, and realistic advice would you give them?

Step 4: Release or Take One Small Action

You cannot solve everything at once. What is one tiny, practical step you can take today? Or is this something completely outside your control that you are ready to let go of?

Mindfulness Breathing & Resets

Simple, powerful Indian pranayama and somatic practices to slow down cognitive spirals and ground your focus.

Anulom Vilom

Alternate Nostril Pranayama
5 Mins

A highly celebrated traditional practice balancing the left and right hemispheres of the brain. Promotes quick focus and calms a racing heart.

Benefit: Sweeps away mental fog and restores deep nervous system equilibrium.

Box Breathing

Four-Square Breathing
5 Mins

A powerful technique used to slow down your breathing and steady your nerves by inhaling, holding, exhaling, and holding for equal counts.

Benefit: Instantly lowers stress and increases calm concentration.

Grounding Exercise

5-4-3-2-1 Sensory Scan
5 Mins

A sensory countdown technique to bring your mind away from anxious thoughts and back to the present physical moment.

Benefit: Interrupts spiraling thoughts and connects you to reality.

Mindful Pause

Simple Awareness
2 Mins

A brief, simple loop of bringing your attention entirely to the physical sensation of your breath without trying to change it.

Benefit: Provides a quick mental reset between tasks or study sessions.
